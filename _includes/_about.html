About Elliot Watts

Hi, present and future fans!

I'm a Science Fiction author, a husband, and a dad to two awesome kids. My family and I currently live in South East Asia. I spend many of my days relaxing under an umbrella on a beach somewhere, but I'm dreaming I'm on a cold starship headed for another world in a solar system far away.

Since I'm not able to do things like that in real life, I write stories that take me to these places in my mind.

Thanks for stopping by and don't forget to sign up for my newsletter to keep up with my book releases and more.
